WISE (the Web-based Interactive Survey Environment) is a system for mounting online surveys, potentially supplemented by mail, telephone, and personal interview modalities. WISE supports the full survey lifecycle including survey development, pilot-testing, recruitment, consent, data collection, and results monitoring.
WISE Advantages

Like many other online survey systems, such as Survey Monkey and REDCAP, WISE supports a variety question types, including open-ended, closed-ended (multiple-choice), and numeric-entry questions with automatic range-checking. However, WISE offers several unique features, including:

       A complete administration interface for tracking surveys through the development & data collection lifecycle.

       Automated online recruitment, managing the complete sequence of email invitations from initial contact through multiple pre-programmed waves followup for non-responders and partial completers.

       Survey authoring using any XML editing program.

       Support for abstraction of survey elements, such as common response sets shared among multiple questions (e.g. Agree ... Disagree), and common questions to be repeated for a set of topics (e.g. to ask the same rating questions across a set of hospitals in a community).

       Flow-control tools including "skip" patterns and logical preconditions that can govern the display of individual questions or entire survey pages.
