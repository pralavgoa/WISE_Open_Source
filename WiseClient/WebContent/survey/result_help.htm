How to interpret results:

• If a button like follows a question, please click the button to view an ordered list of the responses to that question.

•  The percentage of responses is shown for each response category, and your responses are highlighted in yellow. The average (mean) of all responses is shown adjacent to the distribution. The total number of respondents who left the question unanswered is also shown.

For example, the following result would indicate that you rated the difficulty of targeting the Web exercise as a 6 on a 7-point rating scale ranging from "Very easy" to "Very Difficult," and that of the people who answered the question so far, 25% chose the same response that you did. Compared with the overall distribution of responses so far, your self-rating would have been somewhat above the current average rating, which is a 5 on the 7-point scale.
